Slider

Swiper Slider is most modern mobile touch slider with hardware accelerated transitions and amazing native behavior. We've customized it so that you can take advantage of the great Animate.css animation libray. Creating a slider in Alien is really simple task. Just take a slider demo (that suits your need) and replace demo content with your content, that's it!
